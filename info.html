work info

Hey, I’m Bas van Gestel (1990), a creative director, cameraman, editor, illustrator and animator based in ’s-Hertogenbosch, the Netherlands. I am the co-founder and creative director at Kinsmen Collective, a Dutch motion design studio. With Kinsmen, I mostly focus on creating digital illustrations and animations.

Next to my main focus at Kinsmen, I also love to do small independent video projects. I mostly like to do projects with a maximum of 2-3 day shoots with a small crew. I love using as little gear as I can and always try to use natural light when possible. I specialize in small business promotions, travel videos and music videos.

If you would like to work with me, send me a message, give me a call or send me an email. Dutch and English a both fine of course. Cheers!
